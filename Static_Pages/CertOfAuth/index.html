🌟 How To Take Care of Your Jewelry

Special care should be given to all Gemstone & Diamond Jewelry:

  • Avoid: Heat, strong cleaning solutions, ultra-sonic cleaning, and rough mishandling
  • Chemical Protection: Perfume, hair spray, cologne, make-up or other chemicals could damage the micro-porosity of gold
  • Chlorine Warning: Do not let diamonds and gems contact chlorine or bleach as it can pit or discolor the mounting
  • Activity Restrictions: Do not wear jewelry during household or manual labor
  • Impact Protection: Although durable, a hard blow along the grain can chip diamonds and gems
  • Storage: Do not store diamonds and gemstones together as they can scratch each other
  • Moisture Protection: Metal corrosion may occur if exposed to moist or wet conditions

For more specific instructions on how to take care of your jewelry, please visit our website at Malanijewelers.com
