Shipping Information

At Malani Jewelers, we are committed to ensuring your precious jewelry arrives safely and securely. Please review our shipping policies and procedures below to ensure a smooth delivery experience.

Shipping Policy & Product Availability

All orders placed on this site are subject to product availability and will be shipped according to MalaniJewelers.com's shipping policies. We take great care in processing each order to ensure the highest standards of quality and security.

Missing or Damaged Items

Important: In the rare event that your order never arrives, notification of missing or damaged items must be received within 3 days from receipt of the shipment. MalaniJewelers.com reserves the right to request additional information to verify the shipping of high value orders.

Shipping Address Requirements

MalaniJewelers.com only ships to billing addresses for security purposes. If you need an item shipped to a different address, please contact us at info@malanijewelers.com. Our customer service team will work with you to accommodate your shipping needs while maintaining security protocols.

Customer Service & Support

If you are purchasing an item after our business hours, you can send us an email at info@malanijewelers.com and one of our customer service representatives will give you a call as soon as possible.

Credit Card Authorization

To make your checkout process faster, please download our credit card authorization form, fill out all the information, and send it to us through fax or as a scanned image.

Please Note: If a customer has not filled out the credit card authorization form, Malanijewelers.com has the right to withhold the order until its completion.

Shipping FAQ

For security reasons, we typically only ship to billing addresses. However, if you need delivery to a different address, please contact us at info@malanijewelers.com and we'll work with you to find a solution.

You must notify us within 3 days of receiving your shipment if there are any issues with missing or damaged items. Contact us immediately at info@malanijewelers.com.

While not always required, completing the credit card authorization form speeds up your checkout process. For certain orders, we may hold shipment until this form is completed.

You can reach our customer service team by emailing info@malanijewelers.com. If you contact us after business hours, one of our representatives will call you back as soon as possible.

For high-value orders, we reserve the right to request additional information to verify shipping details. This is done to ensure the security of your valuable jewelry purchase.
