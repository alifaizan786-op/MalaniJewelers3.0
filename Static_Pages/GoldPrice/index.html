Understanding Carat Pricing

Gold is measured in carats (K), representing its purity. 24K is pure gold, while 22K, 18K, and 14K have varying levels of alloy mixed in. Jewelry often uses 22K or 18K to improve durability. Our dashboard breaks down pricing by carat to give you better insight into actual retail values.

What the Charts Tell You

The live gold chart updates frequently to reflect market changes in near real-time. The 10-year history chart helps identify long-term trends—crucial for investors looking at gold’s performance during global events like inflation, recessions, and currency fluctuations.

Why Gold Matters

Gold isn't just a precious metal—it's a financial safe haven. When stock markets shake and inflation rises, gold often holds or increases its value. This dashboard gives you clarity on how gold is performing so you can make informed decisions.
