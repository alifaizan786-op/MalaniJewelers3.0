Beyond Global Partnerships

While our partnership with the Aga Khan Foundation represents our primary international commitment, we also believe in supporting our local communities where our stores are located.

  • Local Education Support: Contributing to educational programs in communities where we operate
  • Arts & Culture: Supporting cultural initiatives that celebrate diversity and artistic expression
  • Women's Business Networks: Mentoring and supporting women entrepreneurs in the jewelry and retail sectors
  • Community Events: Participating in and sponsoring local charitable events and fundraisers
