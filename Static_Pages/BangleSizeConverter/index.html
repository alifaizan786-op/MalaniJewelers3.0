No Bangle? No Problem. Use This Method instead

Follow these simple steps to measure your hand and determine your bangle size:

  • Step 1: Form the Bangle Shape

    • Bring your thumb and pinky finger together as if you're slipping on a bangle. This creates the widest part of your hand that a bangle must pass through.

  • Step 2: Measure Your Hand

    • Wrap a flexible measuring tape (or a strip of paper or string) around this widest part. If using paper or string, mark the overlap, then measure that length with a ruler. Thatâ€™s your handâ€™s circumference.

  • Step 3: Find Your Size

    • Use your hand circumference to find the closest match in our chart. Choose the next size up in diameter to ensure a smooth fit.
