Do Not Track (“DNT”) Signals.

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and apps include a Do-Not-Track (“DNT”) setting that you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. No uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. We do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your preference not to be tracked online.
