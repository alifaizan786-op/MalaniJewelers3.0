Frequently Asked Questions

Malani Jewelers has been serving customers since 1897, making us over 125 years old. We are proud to be a fifth-generation family business with more than a century of combined experience in the jewelry industry.

Being a fifth-generation family business means that five generations of the Malani family have been involved in building and operating the company since 1897. Each generation has contributed to our legacy of trust, quality, and exceptional customer service.

Our journey began from a humble store in Atlanta, Georgia, where we laid the foundations of customer service and trust. Atlanta remains our flagship location and headquarters to this day.

We currently operate retail outlets in Atlanta (Georgia), Tampa (Florida), and Dallas (Texas). Additionally, we serve customers nationwide through our online platform at Malanijewelers.com.

Over the decades, we've expanded our global network to include partnerships in London, Belgium, Switzerland, Italy, Malaysia, Singapore, Hong Kong, Beijing, Dubai, Pakistan, and India. This enables us to source the finest jewelry worldwide.

We launched Malanijewelers.com in the 21st century as an industry pioneer, creating a unique online shopping experience that allows customers to access our collections from the convenience of their fingertips.

It has been our honor to serve more than 250,000 satisfied customers throughout our history. We look forward to serving even more customers as we continue our pursuit of business excellence.
