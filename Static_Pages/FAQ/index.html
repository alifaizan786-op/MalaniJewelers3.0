Ordering & Shipping

If your order is received before 2:00 p.m. EST, we ship your order via FedEx the same day. All orders received later will be shipped the following business day. Your order could take up to 10 days with standard shipping and 2-5 days with expedited shipping.

Important shipping notes:

  • We cannot ship to P.O. Box addresses
  • Signature and proof of address required for delivery
  • Real-time tracking available for all orders
  • Flat shipping rate regardless of item shipped

Placing an order is simple and secure:

  • Click 'Add To Basket' for any item you want to purchase
  • Items will appear in your cart on the right-hand side
  • Browse and add more items as desired
  • Click 'CHECKOUT' when ready to complete your purchase
  • Enter customer information and billing details
  • Choose billing address and/or different shipping address
  • Add any special shipping instructions
  • Select from available shipping options
  • Review all order details carefully
  • Enter credit card payment information
  • Complete order and receive confirmation number

You can update quantities, view cart details, and calculate shipping at any time during the process.

After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email if approved. If declined, please call us to re-process. For approved orders, you'll receive another email after 7PM with your FedEx tracking number for real-time tracking.

We currently conduct business only within the 50 states of the United States of America. However, you can arrange for purchases through friends or relatives residing in the U.S., and we will ship to them so they can forward the items to you.

The most common cause is a hold issued by your bank. Simply call your bank to approve the charge, then place the order again. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions about this process.

Sizing & Measurements

Method 1 - Paper Strip:

  • Cut a thin strip of paper
  • Wrap around your finger below the joint, close to the knuckle
  • Mark where the paper meets
  • Measure the distance with a ruler
  • Use our size chart to determine your ring size

Method 2 - Thread:

  • Wrap thread around your finger
  • Measure with ruler
  • Cross-check with ring size chart

Method 3 - Professional: Visit your local jeweler for professional sizing.

Method 1 - Hand Measurement:

  • Place your hand on a steady table
  • Mark a line through the center of your index and pinky knuckles
  • Measure the distance using a scale
  • This is your bangle's inner diameter

Method 2 - Paper/Thread:

  • Cut a thin strip of paper or thread
  • Bring thumb and pinky together
  • Wrap where your hand is widest
  • Mark where paper/thread meets
  • Measure length with ruler
