Return & Exchange Policy – Hassle-Free Jewelry Shopping

You may return merchandise purchased at MalaniJewelers.com if it meets the guidelines below.

How to Return or Exchange Your Jewelry?

If you have any reason to return a part or all of your order, please notify Malani Jewelers Inc. to receive an RMA number. RMA numbers are valid for 15 days from the day they are issued. You must write the RMA number clearly on the outside of the FedEx box and follow all RMA instructions provided.

Timeframe & Conditions for a Successful Return

Final Sale Categories

All sales from our Hot Deals or Deal of the Day categories are final and cannot be returned or exchanged. These items may be slightly inexact in finish, shape, or color from what is described or displayed. No credit will be issued for returns in these categories.

Exchange Policy

MalaniJewelers.com provides a 10-day, no-questions-asked exchange on all products purchased for items of equal or higher value, with the following exceptions:

  • Special order items
  • Gold & silver coins
  • Gold bars
  • Loose stones

To request an exchange, email info@malanijewelers.com with your order number and relevant information.

Timeframe & Conditions for a Successful Return

Return Policy

If you wish to return an item, you may do so within 10 days of purchase. The following conditions apply:

  • A 15% restocking fee will be charged
  • Original shipping and insurance charges will be deducted from your credit
  • All watch sales are final – no returns allowed
  • No calls or return requests will be honored after 10 days

Special Orders & Cancellations

If you cancel or change your mind about a special order, a restocking fee of 25–50% will be charged depending on the situation.

Return Shipping

We will provide a prepaid, insured label for RMA returns. Clients will be responsible for the $25 cost of the label. Exceptions may be made depending on the circumstances. You must notify us by email and receive an RMA number before returning any items.

Layaway Terms

  • 50% down payment is required to initiate layaway
  • All down payments are non-refundable due to price fluctuations and low pricing
  • Items on layaway are not eligible for exchange
  • Jewelry must be paid off within 30 days unless written otherwise
  • If not paid off or picked up on time, payments will be forfeited

Return Requirements

To complete a return or exchange, you must:

  • Notify us within 10 days of receiving your order
  • Return products in new, saleable condition
  • Include all original packaging and secure the item inside and outside the jewelry case/box
  • Cover return shipping costs unless otherwise stated
  • Write the provided RMA number clearly on the outside of the package
