About Malani Jewelers

At Malani Jewelers, we believe jewelry is more than an accessory — it’s an expression of identity, heritage, and celebration. Since our inception, we’ve remained committed to delivering an unmatched luxury shopping experience through our exquisitely designed retail centers and dedicated service team.

A Luxurious Shopping Experience

Each of our retail centers is thoughtfully designed with elegance and comfort in mind. From the moment you step into a Malani Jewelers showroom, you are greeted with warmth and professionalism. Our expertly trained and friendly staff are here to guide you — whether you're shopping for a milestone occasion or building a timeless collection — while building long-lasting relationships founded on trust and personalized service.

The Largest Selection of 22Kt Gold & Diamond Jewelry in North America

We are proud to offer one of the largest curated selections of 22 karat gold, diamond, antique, and gemstone jewelry in North America. Each piece reflects Malani’s standard of excellence and timeless artistry.

Our diamond jewelry is handcrafted with precision, showcasing the brilliance and fire of each stone. Every purchase is backed by our Malani Certificate of Authenticity, ensuring both quality and long-term assurance. We also offer certified diamonds from leading institutions such as GIA, IGI, GAL, and EGL, affirming our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Antique & Ethnic Jewelry – A Nod to Tradition

Our ethnic and antique jewelry collections pay homage to timeless craftsmanship. Designed by master artisans and goldsmiths from around the world, each piece in this collection brings tradition to life, blending intricate detail with enduring beauty.

Natural Gemstones with Astrological Integrity

Explore our vast array of natural birthstones and gemstones, hand-selected by our experienced design and procurement team. Our gemstones are untreated, non-irradiated, and color-true, preserving their natural properties — ideal for those who value their astrological significance.

Men’s Luxury Accessories & Timepieces

Malani Jewelers also offers an exclusive line of men’s accessories, including bold gold chains, rings, and bracelets. For those with a taste for timeless elegance, we carry both unworn and pre-owned luxury watches from Rolex and Cartier — each authenticated, inspected, and sold with certification and warranty.

In-House Jewelry Manufacturing & Expert Repairs

Our in-house team of expert goldsmiths and craftsmen ensures every Malani piece meets our exceptional standards. Whether you're looking to custom design, repair, or resize, you can trust Malani Jewelers to deliver with unmatched expertise.
