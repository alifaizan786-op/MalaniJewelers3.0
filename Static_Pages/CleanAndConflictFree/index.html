Gold Sourcing Policy

Malani, Inc. is deeply concerned about the environmental and social impacts of irresponsible mining practices. We ensure that all our gold suppliers comply with internationally recognized gold sourcing guidelines.

We are committed to sourcing gold, precious metals, and jewelry under the highest Social, Human Rights, and Environmental standards, including compliance with Dodd-Frank regulations and Signet's Responsible Sourcing Standards Protocol (SRSP).

Synthetic Diamond Detection and Disclosure

Malani, Inc. is committed to the fight against undisclosed synthetic diamonds. Our comprehensive detection and disclosure methodology includes:

  1. Advanced Detection Systems: Access to the most effective synthetic diamond detection technology
  2. Trusted Supplier Network: Purchasing exclusively from verified and trusted suppliers
  3. Incident Reporting: Immediate reporting of any undisclosed synthetics to suppliers and relevant parties
  4. Documentation and Tracking: Recording contamination incidents and implementing corrective measures
  5. Risk Classification: Categorizing contamination points into High, Medium, and Low risk levels
  6. Policy Implementation: Establishing clear procedures and testing mechanisms throughout our organization
  7. Supply Chain Assurance: Obtaining compliance declarations from our entire supply chain

Disclosure of Treated Diamonds, Synthetics and Simulants

We adhere to the following essential principles in all transactions involving treated diamonds, synthetics, and simulants:

  • Full Disclosure: Complete and total release of all available information about a diamond and all material steps it has undergone prior to sale
  • No Misrepresentation: Prohibition of terminology misuse or attempts to disguise products in selling, advertising, and distribution
  • Clear Terminology: The word 'diamond' is not used inappropriately in connection with treated diamonds, simulants, or synthetic diamonds
  • Industry Compliance: Full compliance with De Beers' Marketing and Advertising Protocol (MAP) throughout our supply chain

Kimberley Process and System of Warranties

Malani, Inc. is fully committed to complying with all requirements specified in the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme and the World Diamond Council's (WDC) System of Warranties Declaration.

Health and Safety Standards

Malani, Inc. recognizes the need to develop a sustainable, value-creating business and maintains strict health and safety standards:

  • Risk Identification: Systematic review of operations to identify and eliminate health and safety risks
  • Best Practice Standards: Implementation of standards required by prevailing laws and industry best practices
  • Clear Work Practices: Formulation of clearly described work practices and emergency drills
  • Comprehensive Training: All staff trained to adhere to safety work practices and procedures
  • Health Monitoring: Periodic medical checks for staff exposed to hazardous processes
  • Substance-Free Workplace: Zero tolerance for drug, alcohol, or illegal substance abuse
  • Safe Construction: All workplaces constructed to meet or exceed local safety regulations
