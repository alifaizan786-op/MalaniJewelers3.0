Privacy Policy – How Malani Jewelers Protects Your Data

Last Updated: May 3, 2025

Introduction to Our Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices and procedures of Malani Jewelers regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information provided through our website, www.malanijewelers.com. It is intended to inform you of how we handle your data and what choices you have concerning the use of your information. This notice applies exclusively to data collected through our website.

Types of Personal Data We Collect

Information Collection, Usage, and Sharing

Malani Jewelers is the sole proprietor of all data gathered through this website. The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide—whether that’s through completing a form on our website, sending us an email, or communicating with us directly in any other way. This may include your name, contact details, purchase preferences, or any other details you choose to share.

We assure you that we do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to any third parties. The only instance where your information may be shared is with your explicit approval, and only when it directly pertains to fulfilling a transaction or meeting a specific service request related to your jewelry purchases.

The information you share with us is primarily used to respond to your inquiries and to provide services related to your jewelry interests and needs. We may also use this information to improve our offerings, customer service, and overall website experience. Additionally, we may use the data to send you updates about upcoming product launches, special promotions, seasonal collections, pricing changes, or exclusive offers that may be of interest to you.

Should you wish not to receive these communications, you have the right to opt out at any time. Simply contact us via the methods listed on our website or reply to any of our emails requesting removal from our mailing lists.

Please note that Malani Jewelers reserves the right to amend or update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this webpage, and we encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed of how we protect your information.

Your Rights and Access to Personal Data

You are entitled to access the personal information we have on file for you. You can reach out to us at any time to:

  • Review what personal data we have about you
  • Request updates or corrections to your information
  • Ask us to delete any of your information
  • Express any concerns you may have about the handling of your data

We do not share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional use. Text message opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties under any circumstances.

How We Ensure Secure Transactions

Data Security and Protection

The security of your personal data is of utmost importance to us. We take significant precautions to ensure that your information is protected against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. When you submit sensitive information via our website, such as payment details, that information is safeguarded both online and offline.

All sensitive information—especially credit card data—is encrypted and securely transmitted using industry-standard protocols. You can confirm that you are on a secure page by looking for the closed padlock symbol in your web browser or verifying that the page URL begins with "https."

Beyond online encryption, we also enforce strict internal data protection practices. Only employees who require access to your personal information to perform specific tasks—such as customer support, billing, or marketing—are granted permission to view or use such data. All such data is stored within a digitally secured system, protected by appropriate access controls.

Privacy Concerns and Policy Updates

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding our Privacy Policy or your personal information, we encourage you to get in touch with us directly. We are here to assist and ensure that your experience with Malani Jewelers is secure, respectful, and transparent.

Please be aware that Malani Jewelers reserves the right to modify this policy at our discretion. Any revisions will be reflected on this page, and changes will take effect as soon as they are posted. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
