Find Your Ring Size at Home

Shopping online should feel just as magical. Here's how to get your perfect fit the Malani way.

If you already have a ring that fits the intended finger:

  1. Place the ring on a ruler or printable chart.
  2. Measure the inner diameter in millimeters.
  3. Match the diameter to your US ring size.

No ring? No worries. Use this method to measure your finger directly:

  1. Wrap paper or string around the base of your finger.
  2. Mark where it overlaps.
  3. Measure the length in millimeters.
  4. Use the chart below to find your size.
